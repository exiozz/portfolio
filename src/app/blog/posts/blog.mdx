## 🚀 The Starting Point: 2020
It all began back in 2020. During a time when the world was shifting, YouTube opened the doors to **Roblox** for me. Initially, I was a mere observer, fascinated by the infinite diversity of worlds created by others. I spent thousands of hours not just playing, but subconsciously analyzing what made an experience fluid, engaging, and fun. It was my very first contact with digital architecture.

## 🛠️ The Turning Point: 2025
Five years later, the spark happened. I didn't want to just explore other people's worlds anymore; I wanted to build my own. I launched **Roblox Studio** for the first time.

* **Learning to Script**: Understanding that a few lines of code could spawn a bridge or trigger a complex mechanic was my first major technical victory.
* **The "Campus FR" Project**: In mid-2025, this team immersion was my baptism by fire. Collaborating, versioning code, and dealing with the project's eventual abandonment taught me a fundamental lesson: **failure is an integral part of the creative process.** That was when I realized that my passion wasn't the game itself, but the development behind it.

## 🌐 Expanding My Horizons
After the Roblox adventure, I realized that programming is a universal language. I decided to explore the web to become a versatile developer:

> *"If I can build a game, I can build any software."*

1. **HTML & CSS**: To structure the world of the web.
2. **Python**: For automation and core logic.
3. **TypeScript / Next.js**: To build this modern, high-performance portfolio.
