## Overview

Stradaz Café & Bakery is a Roblox café experience where I was responsible for improving both the technical foundation and the overall player experience. Throughout the project, I redesigned multiple user interfaces, developed a second map, reorganized the game's architecture, and resolved several major gameplay issues.

The objective was to create a cleaner, more maintainable project while delivering a smoother and more immersive experience for both players and staff members.


## Responsibilities

* Redesigned and improved several in-game GUI systems.
* Built and integrated a second map into the existing game.
* Refactored and reorganized scripts for better maintainability.
* Fixed major gameplay bugs and improved overall stability.
* Optimized the overall player experience through UI/UX improvements.


## Technologies

* **Roblox Studio** — Game development, environment building, and asset integration.
* **Luau** — Gameplay systems, UI logic, and game scripting.
* **Visual Studio Code + Rojo** — External development workflow and project synchronization.
* **Figma** — UI/UX design and interface prototyping.
* **Trello** — Project planning and task management.


## Future Plans

Stradaz Café & Bakery is still actively evolving. Future updates are planned to further improve the overall experience, including:

* Additional map expansions and new interactive areas.
* More polished and responsive user interfaces.
* New gameplay systems and quality-of-life features.
* Performance optimizations to ensure a smoother experience across all devices.
* Continued bug fixes, balancing, and codebase improvements to support future content.
* Seasonal events and fresh content to keep the experience engaging for the community.


## Results

The project significantly improved the overall quality of the game by delivering a cleaner interface, better-organized systems, increased stability, and additional playable content. These improvements created a more polished experience for players while making future development and maintenance considerably easier.
